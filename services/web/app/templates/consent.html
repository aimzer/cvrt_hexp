CONSENT FORM

Towards An Artificial Visual System Version 1, 12/8/2020

RESEARCHER: Dr. Thomas Serre

  • OBJECTIVE: The objective of this study is evaluating humans' visual reasoning capacities. We ask you to participate in this study as your performance in the task will further or understanding of human's brain. This experiment will be performed by Prolific.
  • PROCEDURES: The experiment consists of a practice session with 3 trials and 6 blocks of 21 trials. In each trial you will be presented with 4 images. Your task is choosing the outlier among the 4 images (the odd one out). You will be asked to rate your confidence over your choice for each trial and given feedback. The task varies in difficulty across blocks and trials, so the choice of a response will require you to analyse details of each image and compare the 4 of them. This experiment requires the use of a mouse or a trackpad!
  • TIME INVOLVED: You can participate in the experiment at your own pace, but typically the session should last about 30 minutes.
  • COMPENSATION: You will receive $4.00 compensation for your time. The level of difficulty varies across tasks, don’t worry if you feel like the task is hard, just try your best!
  • RISKS: There are no anticipated risks for the participants in the current study, though you are free to stop participating at any time.
  • BENEFITS: There are no direct benefits for participating in this study. However, data from this study may improve our knowledge of how the brain processes parses visual scenes. The researchers intend to use data from this study for research projects that may appear in scientific conferences and journals.
  • CONFIDENTIALITY: The data collected from your participation will be anonymous and confidential. By signing this document, you permit us to use anonymized information for scientific purposes including teaching and/or publication. (Use Prolific dialogue here)
